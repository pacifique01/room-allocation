About Us

Built for faster, fairer campus room allocation.

The Room Allocation system helps departments manage room requests without scattered messages, manual follow-ups, or double bookings. Lecturers and representatives submit applications with a selected room, purpose, date, and time; administrators review the queue and keep everyone updated.
